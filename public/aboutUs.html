Our Story

Metasearch for travel? No one was doing it. Until we did.

Since 2004, Time Travel has been revolutionizing the travel industry.

In over a decade, we've grown from a small office of 14 employees into a company of over 1,000 travel-loving teammates working across 7 international brands; KAYAK, SWOODOO, checkfelix, momondo, Cheapflights, Mundi and HotelsCombined. Together, we help people experience the world by creating their favorite travel tools.

Today, we process over 2 billion searches a year for travel information, helping millions of travelers around the globe make confident decisions. With every query, Time Travel searches hundreds of travel sites to show travelers the information they need to find the right flights, hotels, rental cars and vacation packages.

In 2013, we were acquired by Booking Holdings, the world leader in online travel.
